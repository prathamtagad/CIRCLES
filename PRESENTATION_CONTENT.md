## Slide 2: The Problem
**Title:** The Privacy Paradox
**Key Points:**
- **The "All-or-Nothing" Dilemma:** Current platforms force you to share with *everyone* or be completely private.
- **Context Collapse:** You can't separate your professional life, close friends, and acquaintances easily.
- **Data Exploitation:** Users have lost control over their own data and digital footprint.
- **Visual:** A graphic showing a user stressed about posting a photo because their boss, grandma, and best friend are all in the same "Follower" bucket.

**Presenter Script:**
"We live in a privacy paradox. We want to share our lives, but existing platforms force us into an 'all-or-nothing' choice. You either broadcast to the world, or you hide. This 'context collapse' makes us self-censor and creates anxiety."

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Title:** Introducing Circles
**Key Points:**
- **Granular Trust Levels:**
    - 🔒 **Inner Circle:** Encrypted, intimate, for closest friends.
    - 🛡️ **Trusted Circle:** Friends and family.
    - 👥 **Extended Circle:** Acquaintances and professional connections.
- **Context-Aware Sharing:** Choose exactly which circle sees each post.

**Visual:** A diagram of the concentric circles methodology (Inner, Middle, Outer).

**Presenter Script:**
"Enter Circles. We've rebuilt social networking around the natural way humans interact. We don't interact with our boss the same way we do with our best friend. Circles allows you to categorize connections into three distinct trust tiers: Inner, Trusted, and Extended."

---

## Slide 4: Killer Features (The "Wow" Factors)
**Title:** More Than Just Privacy
**Key Points:**
1.  **📊 Privacy Score:** A dynamic, gamified metric that shows how distinct your digital footprint is.
2.  **⚡ Lite Mode:** A sustainability feature that saves bandwidth by hiding media assets on demand.
3.  **🗑️ Right to be Forgotten:** One-click "Delete Account" and "Export Data" built into the core.
4.  **👁️ Transparency Dashboard:** Real-time stats on data requests (we show 0 because we sell nothing).

**Visual:** Screenshots of the Privacy Dashboard and the Lite Mode toggle demo.

**Presenter Script:**
"But we didn't stop at groups. We introduced the 'Privacy Score' to gamify digital hygiene. We added 'Lite Mode' for accessibility and bandwidth saving in developing regions. And most importantly, we give users a 'Kill Switch'—true ownership to delete or export their data instantly."
