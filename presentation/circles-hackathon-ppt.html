The Privacy Paradox 🚨

🔓

All-or-Nothing

Current platforms force you to share with everyone or be completely invisible. There's no middle ground.

😰

Context Collapse

Your boss, grandma, and best friend see the same content. This creates anxiety and self-censorship.

📊

Data Exploitation

Users have lost control over their digital footprint. Your data is the product being sold.
